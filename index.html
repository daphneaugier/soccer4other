Who are we?

Soccer4Other


Soccer4Other is a social start-up that aims to use sport values as drivers of social change by organizing soccer tournaments for students. We have previously organized tournaments in Montréal, Mexico and France.

To foster this social change, we dedicate all of our profits to local charities.

VISION

Use sports values as drivers of social change.

MISSION

Develop soccer activities for students in developed part of the world and use 100% of our profits to foster education through sport in underprivileged areas.

TODAY

We organise soccer tournaments for students in Montreal and give out all of our profits to Pour 3 Point.

BUSINESS PHILOSOPHY

The rule of "the strongest takes all" must come down. Offering hope and opportunity for a better future is what drives our organization.

VALUE PROPOSITION

S4O gives the opportunity to play soccer while contributing to foster education through sport in the underprivileged areas of the world.
