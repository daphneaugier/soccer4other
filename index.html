Who are we?

Soccer4Other is a social start-up that organize socccer tournaments to support education through sport in underpriviledged areas of the world. We have previously organized tournaments in Montréal, Mexico and France. To foster this social change, we dedicate all of our profits to local charities that use sports values as drivers of social change.

Offering hope and opportunity for a better future is what drives our organization
